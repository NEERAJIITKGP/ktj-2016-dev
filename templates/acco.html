Instructions

Payment through Online Payment System:
Participants will have to make online payment of Rs. 1100 (Rs. 100/- is as a caution money for all the accommodation materials provided which will be refunded during the fest  ).  If you  are coming in a team (say 5 membered team), payment must be done separately from all the 5 accounts.

Payment through Demand Draft:
Participants will have to make and bring along with them a draft of Rs. 1100/- (Rs. 100/- is as a caution money for all the accommodation materials provided which will be refunded after the fest . ) in favour of "KSHITIJ", payable at Kharagpur (0202) [SBI Bank Code]. If you are coming in a team (say 5 membered team), a single draft can be drawn for the whole amount (5 x 1100 = Rs. 5500).
However, all team members are required to fill the form separately. If they have a common DD then the same DD number is to be entered in all the forms. If you dont want to avail accommodation facility, then also you need to provide a draft of Rs. 1100/- in order to participate in any of the event.

If you don’t want to avail accommodation facility, then also you need to make an online payment of Rs. 1100/- in order to participate in any of the event. Payment can be made either through Demand Draft or Online.

Money is non-refundable.
Accommodation will ONLY be provided to those, who provide the online payment details in the Accommodation form. 

Participants won’t be allowed to attend any Events,Guest Lectures, Workshops and Megashows without registering in the finance tent, but accommodation will only be provided to the ones whose accommodation request has been approved online. 

Accommodation will be provided in a first come, first serve basis. Participants are required to update their accommodation forms with the PNR number of their travelling ticket. 

Checklist of items that must be brought during the registration at IIT Kharagpur:
1.College Identity Card
2.Accommodation Form Printout 
3.Ticket received from TownScript


What Not To Do

  • The Demand Draft should be made in favour of 'KSHITIJ' and not IIT KHARAGPUR or KSHITIJ 16 or any other entity.
Instructions

  1. Accommodation for Kshitij Participants in IIT Kharagpur will start from 21st January. Participants coming before that will have to arrange accommodation on their own.
  2. After registering for accommodation, Kshitij team will take three days to process your request for accommodation.
  3. He/She must fill the actual details in the form as in the college identity card.
  4. Participants are requested to get their accommodation forms printed after their accommodation status is approved.
  5. Accommodation will be provided to students on first come first serve basis. All the finalists must register for Accommodation. They will be granted Accommodation as soon as they register.
  6. If students from a particular college are coming in a group then one letter with the names of all students in it will suffice.
  7. Accommodation Fees once Paid is non-Refundable.


FAQ

Q. HOW DO I REACH KHARAGPUR? 

The KGP station is connected to all major cities of the country by train. Just hop on! If your city does not have a direct rail route to Kharagpur, then catch a train to the City of Joy- Kolkata. KGP is situated 120 Kms off Kolkata which is well connected to all the cities of India by rail and air. Train from Kolkata to Kharagpur are available round the clock. The list of local trains from Kolkata to KGP can be obtained from here . 

Q. HOW TO REACH THE IIT CAMPUS ON ARRIVAL AT KHARAGPUR? 

The first thing that you will notice on coming out of the KGP station will be the Participant's Reception (PR) team. (We believe in Atihi Devo Bhava, besides there isn't anything else worth noting about the KGP station) We will be there 24x7 from 1800 hrs, 21st of January. Periodic announcements will be made at the station addressing the participants. IIT KGP buses and the volunteers will be available to bring you to the campus. The volunteers will guide you to the Kshitij arena- The Vikramshila Complex. Moreover you can also take a cab to the IIT Campus. The fare from station to the IIT campus is Rs. 120 to Rs 150 (depends on your negotiation skills) and it is a 10 minutes ride. 

Q. WHAT AFTER REACHING THE KSHITIJ ARENA? 

You will be taken to the Control Tent where you and your team/ contingent will have to register. For the registration you will have to bring the printout of the accommodation form that has been put up on the website. After that you will be provided accommodation in the campus. There will be nominal registration fee. There will also be fully refundable security deposit. 

Q. WHAT DOCUMENTS DO I HAVE TO BRING IN ORDER TO PARTICIPATE OR TO AVAIL ACCOMMODATION? 

You have to bring the printout of the accommodation form in order to avail the accommodation during Kshitij. It is of absolute importance that you carry your Institute Identity Card with you. Apart from this, participants may be required to bring a few validating documents for some events if the Event rules require them to do so. Please read the rules of the events very carefully. 

Q. ALRIGHT, I AM REGISTERED AT THE CONTROL TENT. WHAT NEXT? WHERE DO I STAY? 

Participant will be accommodated in the Hostels of IIT. We have 18 Boy's Hostels and 6 Girl's Hostels and a number of Bachelor Flats. The boys and Girls will be accommodated separately in a dormitory sort of arrangement. 

Q. WHAT SHOULD I BRING WITH ME? 

The participants will be provided with mattresses and blankets so you need not stuff your backpack with these items. You MUST carry your institute Id card with you. Participants of various events should bring anything that is required for their respective events. Regarding this, you can contact the respective coordinators of the events. 

Q. I AM REALLY EXCITED TO COME DOWN FOR KSHITIJ. WHEN CAN I COME AT THE EARLIEST? 

Accommodation shall be provided to participants reaching Kharagpur after 1800 hrs on 21st January 2015. We advise you to stick with this scheme. However, if you cannot help it and have a genuine problem, get in touch with Team Kshitij and we shall take care of it. 

Q. WHERE DO I EAT? 

The Kshitij arena will have a lot of food stalls that will take care of your appetite. Of course, you will have to pay for that but they will be subsidized. There are a lot of eating joints in the campus - CCD, Tikka, Sahara, Veggies, Billoo's etc. which go pretty easy on your pocket. There are some restaurants outside the campus as well. Besides, all the hostels (or halls as we call them) have Nescafe outlets. 

Q. I HAVE A GOOD FRIEND AT KGP. IS THERE A CHANCE I CAN PUT UP WITH MY FRIEND? 

Of course! If you have a good friend we won't play spoilsport. You can always put up with your friends and indulge in the camaraderie. But registration in the site and for accommodation is mandatory. 

Q. WHAT CONDUCT DO YOU EXPECT OF ME? 

During Kshitij, the otherwise serene campus is transformed into a vibrant hub with students from more than 1300 colleges coming together. However, it is required that you follow a proper code of conduct. Any instance of hooliganism, indecent behavior and rowdiness will not be tolerated. If found indulging in any misconduct, the participant shall be asked to leave immediately along with his team/ contingent. 

Q. WHAT ABOUT THE SECURITY OF MY BELONGINGS INSIDE THE CAMPUS? 

IIT KGP has round the clock security everywhere in the campus. Additional guards will be posted at the hostels to safeguard your belongings. Volunteers will also be present at the accommodation venues. All said and done, we request you to avoid carrying expensive item. 

Q. IN CASE OF ANY HEALTH PROBLEM, WHERE SHOULD I GO? 

We have a medical hospital- the BC ROY Hospital inside the campus that will take care of your medical problems, if any. In case of any illness, you or your team members can contact the control tent and we shall ensure immediate action. 

Q. WHEN WILL I GET MY PRIZES AND CERTIFICATES? 

The winners will get their prizes and certificates on the last day of Kshitij during the Prize distribution ceremony. Those who need to leave early can collect it from control tent at the end of the day. 

Q. HOW DO I CHECK OUT? 

For checking out, you need to go to control tent and inform them. Accommodation volunteers will be present at various halls at preassigned times who will collect the blankets, mattresses etc. issued against your name. Only after that you can collect your security deposits from the control tent. 

Q. WHEN DO I CHECK OUT? 

The last slot for collection of accommodation materials by accommodation volunteers is 24th January 2016, 18:00hrs. Accommodation volunteers will not be available after this time. Those leaving next morning can only wrap up the formalities at 7am. 

Q. I STILL HAVE A FEW UNANSWERED QUESTIONS, WHERE CAN I LOOK FOR ANSWERS?

You can always use the forum for any queries you have. You can use the private queries section as well. Or else, you can also contact team Kshitij for any queries . The contacts are available on the contacts page. You can mail your queries to sidhant.agarwal@ktj.in.

Q. I HAVE WITH ME MY HARDWARE DESIGNS WHICH WOULD NEED REGULAR POWER SUPPLY TO WORK WITH. DO I GET PROVISIONS OF POWER PLUGS? 

Power Plugs are not much in number and hence all the work related to power, if possible should be completed before you come over here to Kgp.
