The event will have two rounds.

Round 1:
Online submission Round.
The participant teams have to come up with a unique campaign to promote and eradicate the root cause of malnutrition. They have to submit an abstract containing preliminary ideas of how they will execute the campaign and how effective their method is towards this problem.
  1. Deadline for the abstract submission: 27th December 2015.
  2. Maximum Team Size: 5
  3. The Parting teams have to design a poster and ideate slogan for their own-formulated campaign.
  4. Abstract is to be mailed at submissions@ktj.in.

Round 2:
Presentation round.
  1. Teams selected on the basis of abstract will give presentation on the idea of the campaign in front of the judges.
  2. There would be a time limit for the presentation (12 min.) followed by Question and Answer session by judges (3 min.)
  3. The presentation should contain detailed execution, further scope of study and other details.
